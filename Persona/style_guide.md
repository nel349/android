## Sentence Structure
[Describe your preferred sentence patterns and rhythm]
Basic Patterns (Building Blocks)
S + V (Subject + Verb): Birds fly.
S + V + Adjective (Predicate Adjective): The dog was happy.
S + V + Noun (Subject Complement): He is a firefighter. 

Vary sentence lengths throughout writing. Make it easy to read. Make sound natural.
